I believe AI is a powerful tool — but a tool like any other. This page lays out how I use it on this website, and where I draw the lines. The short version: the words and ideas here are mine.

## Content

- **None of the writing on this site is written by AI** unless I explicitly say so. The articles, notes, and pages are mine — drafted, edited, and signed off by a human.
- I do use LLMs for **copy-editing, proofreading, grammar, and spelling checks** — much the same way I'd use a thesaurus or a grammar checker. The substance and voice remain mine.
- I occasionally use AI to generate **dummy or example content** in places where I would previously have used Lorem Ipsum or written placeholder text myself.
- I generally **avoid AI-generated imagery** and prefer to attribute the human creator of any image I use. On the rare occasions I do use an AI-generated image, I will do my best to clearly mark it as such.

## Code

- I use AI coding agents (mostly [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code)) to help build and maintain this site. I'm not vibe-coding — I care about the code quality, the architecture, and the patterns. I review what gets committed.
- Whether AI helped or not, the code on this site is **owned and authored by me** and I take responsibility for it. The source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/dannysmith/dannyis-astro) if you want to look.
