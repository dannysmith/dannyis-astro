## 7. Overrides

`title` and `description` props override the values fetched from the JSON. Useful when the canonical title is too generic for the embedding context.

<LCVid
  src="lcdemo-part-2"
  title="A more specific title for this article"
  description="A description tailored to the surrounding prose, not the one I wrote in the LoomClone admin."
/>

## 8. Display toggles — minimal
